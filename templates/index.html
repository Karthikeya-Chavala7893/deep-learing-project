Understanding diabetic retinopathy and eye health is crucial for prevention

👀

What is Diabetic Retinopathy?

A diabetes complication affecting the eyes. High blood sugar damages blood vessels in the retina, potentially leading to vision loss if untreated.

⚠️

Warning Signs

  • Blurred or fluctuating vision
  • Dark spots or floaters
  • Difficulty seeing colors
  • Vision loss in advanced stages
🛡️

Prevention Tips

  • Control blood sugar levels
  • Annual comprehensive eye exams
  • Manage blood pressure
  • Quit smoking
  • Healthy diet & exercise

Early Detection Saves Sight

Don't wait for symptoms. Regular screening can detect problems before vision loss occurs.
